Hunde Inc. Adult Foster Home is licensed to provide care for individuals with intellectual and developmental disabilities. Our home is located in Milwaukie, OR. We maintain the highest standards of care and comfort for our residents. Our home is also equipped with all the necessary items, including two walk-in and roll in showers, fire sprinkler system, individual sinks and TVs in all our bedrooms, a ramp and a wheelchair accessible van to transport those in wheelchairs. We meet and exceed all the requirements of the Clackamas County program for adults with intellectual and developmental disabilities.

Our 24/7 staff always provides personalized treatment by qualified professionals with great attention to detail, ready to meet the needs of our residents. Hunde Inc. AFH is a place where you feel at home, safe and secure. Your comfort and meeting your physical and emotional needs are our priorities.

Our staff are trained to enhance the lives of individuals with developmental disabilities by creating a warm, happy living environment. We believe in having a healthy lifestyle and diet, so we are constantly finding creative ways to incorporate exciting healthy foods and physical activities in our residents daily routine.

We work hard to make sure our home is a place where everyone feels at home. We would love to hear from you if you have interest and/or would like to find out more about us.

Feel free to call or email us and we will respond promptly.
